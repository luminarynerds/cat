What is cost-per-checkout? This is a simple ROI proxy: total dollars spent on items in a subject area divided by total checkouts. Lower is better — it means each checkout "costs" less. This helps identify subjects where spending is high but usage is low (potential reallocation candidates) and subjects where spending is efficient. Note: this only includes items that have price data in your catalog export.
