How your data fields were detected from the uploaded file

What is this page? When you uploaded your file, the tool tried to match your column headers to the fields it knows how to analyze. This page shows you the results. A Full badge means almost every row has data for that field. A Empty badge means your file didn't include that data (or used a column name the tool didn't recognize).

Empty fields are totally fine — the reports simply skip any analysis that requires missing data. For the richest analysis, the most important fields are Call Number, Publication Year, and Checkouts.
