What is MUSTIE weeding? MUSTIE is a checklist that helps you decide whether to remove an item from your shelves. Each letter stands for a reason to weed: Misleading (outdated facts), Ugly (worn out), Superseded (newer edition exists), Trivial (no lasting value), Irrelevant (doesn't fit your community), Elsewhere (available from another source).

The tool flags items that might match these criteria based on age, checkout data, and condition. You make the final call.

Why this matters: Regular weeding keeps your collection fresh, makes browsing easier, and frees up space and budget for new material.
