What am I looking at? This report shows how your collection is actually being used. The Avg per Item tells you the typical number of checkouts across all items (including items that have never circulated). The Median is often more useful because it isn't skewed by a few very popular items — if the median is 0 or 1, that means the majority of your collection has barely circulated.

The Top Circulating Items list can help you identify titles that may need replacement copies or that indicate strong patron interest in a particular subject.
