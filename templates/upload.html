Import Data

Upload a CSV or Excel file exported from your ILS

How do I get this file? In your ILS (Sierra, Polaris, Koha, Evergreen, etc.), go to the Reports or Create Lists area and pull a list of all items at your branch. Then export/download that list as a CSV or Excel file. If you're not sure how, ask your consortium's system administrator — they can usually generate one for you.
  • The file should have one row per item (or one row per bib record).
  • Include as many columns as possible — the tool automatically matches them.
  • At minimum, include Title, Call Number, and Publication Year.
  • For circulation reports, also include a Checkouts (or Total Circs) column.

What Happens After Upload?

1

Column matching

The tool scans your column headers and matches them to standard fields (Title, Author, Call Number, etc.). It understands dozens of common variations, so you don't need to rename anything.

2

Data cleanup

Publication years, checkout counts, and dates are automatically cleaned up and converted to the right format. For example, "c2019" or "2019." both become 2019.

3

Subject classification

Call numbers are read to determine the subject area of each item. This works with both Library of Congress (LC) and Dewey Decimal call numbers.
