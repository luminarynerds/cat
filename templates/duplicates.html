Duplicate Detection

Items that may be unintentional duplicates based on ISBN or title and author

How this works: This report flags two kinds of duplicates:
  • ISBN matches: Items sharing the exact same ISBN — these are almost certainly the same edition. Multiple copies may be intentional (high-demand titles) or accidental.
  • Title + Author matches: Items with the same title and author but possibly different ISBNs. These could be different editions, different formats (hardcover vs. paperback), or unintentional duplicates.
Review each group to decide whether extra copies are warranted by demand or should be consolidated.
