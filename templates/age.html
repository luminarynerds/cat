What does this tell me? This chart shows how many items you have from each decade. A healthy collection typically has a strong showing in the most recent decades, with older decades tapering off — though this depends on your library's focus. If you see a huge spike in the 1990s but very little from the 2020s, that's a sign that new material hasn't been replacing aging stock.

The Avg Checkouts column can be revealing: if older decades have very low average checkouts, those items may be sitting on shelves unused.
