Collection Gaps

Potential holes and weaknesses in your collection

What does this report show? This is the core report for collection development. It identifies four types of gaps:
  • Underrepresented Subjects — subject areas that make up a very small percentage of your collection compared to others.
  • Subjects Lacking Recent Material — areas where you have very few (or zero) items published in the last 5 years.
  • Aging Areas — subjects where the typical item is more than 15 years old, meaning the section may contain outdated information.
  • Non-Circulating Items — items that have never been checked out at all.
Not every gap is a problem — some areas are intentionally small at certain libraries. Use this as a starting point for conversations with your director or collection development team.
